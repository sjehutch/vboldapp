ProFumeÆ gas fumigant is used to control postharvest insect pests in food facilities. Critical to the effective use and safe stewardship of this fumigant is the ProFume Fumiguideô. This application is based on Precision Fumigation, defined as optimizing fumigant use for maximum efficiency and safety. The ProFume Fumiguide calculates precise dosages on the target pest and environmental conditions, and then provides refined fumigation instructions based on gas monitoring data.
